Who am I?

I’m Bap, a Roblox systems developer who likes building mechanics that are fast, clear, and fun to play with. I’ve been around Roblox development for years, and I care most about the stuff players actually feel: movement, feedback, progression, and the moment-to-moment loop.

How I work

I like modular code, readable controllers, reusable services, and systems that can survive when a client changes their mind halfway through the project. I move fast, but I still try to keep the code understandable.

  • TweenService, RunService, Debris
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • C++, Java, XML, Squirrel
